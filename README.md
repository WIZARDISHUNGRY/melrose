## Note notation

| Notation | Alternative | Description 
|----------|-------|-------------
| C4       | ¼C,C,c  | quarter C octave 4 
| 2E5      | ½E5,½e5 | Halftone (2 x ¼) E octave 5
| 1C       |        | Full tone C octave 4
| F#       | F♯,f♯  | F sharp
| G_       | G♭    | G flat
| G.       | G.    | duration x 1.5 
| =        | =     | quarter rest
| 2=       | ½=    | half rest
| 1=       | 1=    | full rest


## Sequence notation

| Notation    | Description 
|-------------|---
| C D E F       | 4 quarter tones
| (C E) (d5 f5) | 2 doublets
